U.S. Army War College Innovation Marketplace

The U.S. Army War College Innovation Marketplace is an online marketplace linking your inquiry or request for assistance with USAWC expertise. The graduate-level students, faculty and staff can potentially offer wide array of analytical and planning expertise -- in leadership, logistics, national security, futures and more.

Identify a problem, area of interest, or query -- and the USAWC team will assess our ability to assist or act.

We invite you to pose your question or inquiry - with some specificity and your sense of the timeline for this inquiry.
