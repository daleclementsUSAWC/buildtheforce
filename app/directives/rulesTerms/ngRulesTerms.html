Terms

How Net Power is Calculated: (AC + RC Total Power)*(Readiness Factor)*(Sustainment Factor)*(Technology Factor)*(Shortfall Penalty).

Power Ratio: Your Army's total net power (off. + def.) divided by crisis total power (off. + def.).

Total Power: Active Component (AC) + Reserve Component (RC) power (off. and/or def.)

RC Power: (RC total) * (RC Capability Factor).

Readiness Factor: Your overall % funding for MILCON, MILPERS, O&M, and Procurement.

Sustainment Factor: Your Army's total enabler capability (AC + (RC * RC Capability Factor)) divided by the total enabler requirement (Army total + any additional from crisis).

Technology Factor: Based on whether your technology level is higher or lower than crisis level.

Shortfall Penalty: Reduce your net power by 50% (multiply total power by 0.50) if you do not have both types of crisis priority forces or your combined qty of these forces < (round # + crisis tech lvl).
